ADMIN HELP PAGE

Managing Courses

Adding a New Course: Go to 'Courses' and click 'Add New Course'. Fill in the details and click 'Submit'.

Editing a Course: Click 'Edit' next to the course, make changes, and click 'Save'.

Deleting a Course: Click 'Delete' next to the course and confirm the deletion.

Managing Users

Adding a New User: Go to 'Users' and click 'Add New User'. Fill in the details and click 'Submit'.

Editing a User: Click 'Edit' next to the user, make changes, and click 'Save'.

Deleting a User: Click 'Delete' next to the user and confirm the deletion.

Viewing Enquiries

Viewing Enquiries: Go to 'Enquiries'. You can filter by date, course, or user.

Responding to Enquiries: Click on an enquiry to see details and respond if needed.
